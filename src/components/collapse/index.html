Steve Jobs
Steve Jobs (Born 24 February 1955 in San Francisco, California) is an American inventor, entrepreneur and co-founder of Apple inc
Steve Gary Wozniak
Stephen Gary Wozniak was an American computer engineer who co-founded Apple Computer with Steve Jobs. Steve Gary Wozniak attended the University of Colorado and later transferred to UC Berkeley, where he received his undergraduate degree in electrical Engineering and Computing (EECS) in 1987.
Jonathan Ive
Sir Jonathan Ive is an industrial designer and senior vice President of Design at Apple. He has been involved in the design of the iPod, iMac, iPhone, iPad and many other Apple products. Aside from Steve Jobs, he was the most influential person on Apple's famous products.
